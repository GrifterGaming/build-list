About This Build

Your space is your haven — making it your own is what this is all about. Mine is a shared room that doubles as an office and gaming area. The goal from the start was to avoid the RGB-drenched streamer aesthetic you see everywhere online, and instead build something warm and inviting — a place to unwind, create, and actually enjoy being in.

Walnut tones, low amber lighting, and a shelf of books and small decor pieces give the room its anchor. From there, every piece I added had to earn its spot — nothing chrome, nothing loud, nothing that clashes with that feel.

Nothing on this page is staged or sponsored. Every item shown is something I actually use, sitting exactly where it sits in the room.
